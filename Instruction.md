## Overview

Get ready to dive deep into data and tackle mental health challenges at the **Make-With-Data Hackathon**.

The **Make-With-Data initiative** supports research on community-based challenges, primarily related to:

- Local conservation  
- Sustainability  
- Mental health  

Through hands-on workshops and activities, participants explore real-world problems using **data sources and scientific methods**. The program is designed to both:

- Investigate meaningful community issues  
- Teach important **mapping and data science skills**

---

## Project Background

**DNM Geospatial** is collaborating with **Edenas Children** to develop a baseline understanding of the **state of mental health in New York City**.

The research examines how mental health correlates with several factors, including:

- Environmental variables  
- Socioeconomic conditions  
- Demographic characteristics  

The **“Make-With-Data: Mapping Mental Health” workshop series** culminates in this hackathon event.

Participants will create **maps that visualize the collected research data**, helping the community better understand the mental health landscape of NYC.

The goal is not only to share findings but also to **empower community members with mapping and data analysis skills**.
